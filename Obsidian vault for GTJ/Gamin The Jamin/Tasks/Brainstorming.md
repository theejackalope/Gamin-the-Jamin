- ### nostalgia runner a parkour road trip
  Player is playing a character doing parkour outside the window of a moving car. Maybe a road trip across Canada (Only a few provinces to start), Main character is moving with a box van? Game is a 2D auto scrolling platformer. Pros: Relatively simple, We control the time each level takes (music will be easier), 2D. Cons: Simple, 2D platformer (probs going to be a few).
- road trip around canada
- provincial themes for each level.
- day night cycle (level starts at dawn and goes into night) **low level priority**
- paralax background (city sky lines of major cities in level)
- main menu is a gameboy
- art style gba games styles
- add hats for cosmetics
- not more "toonies" and "loonies" in each level than the cost of the cosmetic
- bonus level (far away) train travel, mattress on car roof to jump into clouds and hop on clouds (clouds have more coins)
- inbetween levels have a place to buy items/consumables
- power ups (extra life, speed boost (includes camera speed and character speed), magnet)
- level results are an "hours" sign with the coins collected, time it took, other stuff. and then to go "inside" the gas station click the handle to open
- obstacles are themed to each province level but the basic obstacles are: fence, car, bush, tree, rocks, signs, street light, semi with trailer, farm houses, barns, billboards, wildlife (deer, cows, coyotes, etc), marsh (slows you down, jump to get out), power poles, silos, wind turbines (alberta only),
- road trip path, YK, BC, AB, SK, NWT, NU, MB, ON, QC, NB, PEI, NS, NL
- difficulty settings, change camera and player speed
